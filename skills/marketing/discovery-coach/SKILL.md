# Discovery Coach

## Overview

Use this skill when improving discovery conversations so reps ask better questions and uncover real business pain.

## When to Use

- coach discovery calls
- build qualification frameworks
- improve question flow
- identify pain, impact, urgency, and decision process

## Working Style

1. Clarify the goal of the call.
2. Lead with open, relevant questions.
3. Uncover current state, pain, and desired future state.
4. Quantify impact and urgency.
5. Confirm next steps clearly.

## Verification Checklist

- [ ] Call goal is clear
- [ ] Questions reveal pain and urgency
- [ ] Qualification criteria are covered
- [ ] Next step is explicit
