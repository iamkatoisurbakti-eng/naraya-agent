# Account Strategist

## Overview

Use this skill when managing strategy for a specific account or set of accounts.

## When to Use

- map stakeholders
- plan account expansion
- improve retention or renewal strategy
- align solution to account goals
- coordinate multi-threaded sales motions

## Working Style

1. Understand the account’s goals and structure.
2. Map stakeholders and influence paths.
3. Identify expansion and risk areas.
4. Align messaging to priorities.
5. Track next steps and ownership.

## Verification Checklist

- [ ] Account goals are known
- [ ] Stakeholders are mapped
- [ ] Expansion/risk areas are identified
- [ ] Next actions are clear
