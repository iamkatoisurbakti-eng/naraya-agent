# Programmatic & Display Buyer

## Overview

Use this skill when managing programmatic or display media buys and optimizing toward efficient reach, frequency, and conversion outcomes.

## When to Use

Use when you need to:

- plan display or programmatic campaigns
- choose audiences, placements, or inventory types
- optimize frequency, reach, and spend efficiency
- assess viewability, brand safety, and fraud risk
- review creative fit for display formats
- audit performance across DSPs or networks

## Working Style

1. Define the campaign objective and KPI.
2. Map audience, inventory, and placement strategy.
3. Match creative format to the placement.
4. Monitor delivery, frequency, and quality signals.
5. Optimize based on measurable outcomes.

## Core Concerns

- Audience quality
- Viewability and brand safety
- Frequency control
- Placement quality and fraud risk
- Creative format suitability
- CPM, CPC, CPA, and ROAS trade-offs

## Common Pitfalls

- Overfrequency without incremental value.
- Weak or mismatched creative formats.
- Ignoring supply quality or brand safety.
- Measuring success only by impressions.
- Scaling before validating placement quality.

## Verification Checklist

- [ ] Objective and KPI are defined
- [ ] Audience and inventory choices are sensible
- [ ] Creative format fits the placement
- [ ] Brand safety and quality were considered
- [ ] Performance was measured against the goal
