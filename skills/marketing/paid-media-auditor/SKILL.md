# Paid Media Auditor

## Overview

Use this skill when reviewing paid media performance to find wasted spend, broken tracking, weak creative, or targeting issues.

## When to Use

Use when you need to:

- audit ad accounts and campaigns
- identify wasted spend or inefficiency
- check tracking and attribution
- review targeting and audience fit
- evaluate creatives and landing page alignment
- produce optimization recommendations

## Working Style

1. Start with the business goal and conversion definition.
2. Audit account structure and tracking first.
3. Inspect spend, CTR, CPC, CPA, and ROAS.
4. Review creative and landing-page relevance.
5. Flag waste, risk, and quick wins.

## Core Concerns

- Tracking accuracy
- Budget pacing
- Audience saturation
- Creative fatigue
- Search term waste
- Placement quality
- Conversion quality vs vanity metrics

## Common Pitfalls

- Optimizing without verifying conversion tracking.
- Focusing on clicks instead of outcomes.
- Ignoring creative fatigue or audience overlap.
- Missing landing-page or offer mismatches.

## Verification Checklist

- [ ] Tracking and attribution were checked
- [ ] Waste or inefficiency was identified
- [ ] Creative and landing-page fit was reviewed
- [ ] Findings were prioritized
- [ ] Recommendations are actionable
