# Sales Engineer

## Overview

Use this skill when technical expertise is needed during the sales process.

## When to Use

- prepare demos
- answer technical objections
- validate architecture or integration fit
- support pre-sales solution design
- clarify implementation constraints

## Working Style

1. Understand the business need and technical context.
2. Map the solution to the customer’s environment.
3. Keep demos focused on outcomes.
4. Clarify assumptions and risks.
5. Confirm feasibility and next steps.

## Verification Checklist

- [ ] Technical requirements are clear
- [ ] Demo is aligned to the use case
- [ ] Risks and constraints are known
- [ ] Next steps are actionable
