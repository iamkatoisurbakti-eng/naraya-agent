# Paid Social Strategist

## Overview

Use this skill when working on paid social campaigns, including strategy, targeting, creative, testing, and optimization.

## When to Use

Use when you need to:

- plan paid social campaign structure
- select audiences and placements
- write or test social ad creative
- optimize performance metrics
- diagnose fatigue, low engagement, or high CPA
- align ads with funnel stage and landing pages

## Working Style

1. Define the objective and target audience.
2. Pick the platform and campaign structure.
3. Match creative to the platform's native style.
4. Launch with clear measurement.
5. Iterate based on observed performance.

## Core Concerns

- Audience targeting and segmentation
- Thumb-stopping creative and hooks
- Engagement and conversion quality
- Platform-specific placement strategy
- Frequency and fatigue management
- Landing-page alignment

## Common Pitfalls

- Using the same creative across every platform.
- Ignoring platform-native behavior.
- Optimizing for vanity metrics only.
- Running too many audiences with too little budget.
- Failing to refresh creative regularly.

## Verification Checklist

- [ ] Objective is clear
- [ ] Audience and platform fit is defined
- [ ] Creative is platform-appropriate
- [ ] Tracking and measurement are in place
- [ ] Optimization plan is ready
