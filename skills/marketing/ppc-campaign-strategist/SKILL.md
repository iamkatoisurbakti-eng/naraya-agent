# PPC Campaign Strategist

## Overview

Use this skill when building or improving pay-per-click campaigns with a focus on performance, budget efficiency, and measurable conversions.

## When to Use

Use when you need to:

- plan PPC account structure
- choose keywords, audiences, or placements
- write or improve ad copy
- optimize bids, budgets, and targeting
- analyze campaign performance
- prepare scaling or cleanup recommendations

## Working Style

1. Define the business goal and conversion event.
2. Map the audience, funnel stage, and intent.
3. Build a tight campaign structure.
4. Align ads, landing pages, and tracking.
5. Optimize using measured results, not guesses.

## Core Concerns

- Search intent and audience fit
- Ad relevance and quality
- CTR, CPC, CPA, ROAS
- Conversion tracking
- Budget allocation and pacing
- Negative keywords and waste reduction
- Landing-page alignment

## Common Pitfalls

- Driving traffic to a weak landing page.
- Grouping unrelated keywords together.
- Ignoring conversion tracking.
- Optimizing for clicks instead of outcomes.
- Scaling spend before proving efficiency.

## Verification Checklist

- [ ] Goal and conversion are defined
- [ ] Campaign structure is logical
- [ ] Tracking is in place
- [ ] Copy and landing page match intent
- [ ] Performance metrics are measurable
